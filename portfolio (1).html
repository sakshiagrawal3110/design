Design Approach

How I work

01 — Research
🔍
Evidence First
Every design decision starts with a question. I use mixed-methods research — interviews, surveys, diary studies, and analytics — to build a complete picture before touching Figma.
02 — Systems
⬡
Think in Systems
Good design scales. I design tokens, patterns, and components that work across surfaces, brands, and contexts — not just the one screen in front of me.
03 — AI-Native
✦
AI as a Medium
I treat AI not as a feature but as a new design medium — with its own failure modes, trust dynamics, and interaction patterns that require a new design vocabulary.
